🚗 Indian Used Car Market Intelligence Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Tableau dashboard that analyzes the Indian used car market using more than 37,000 vehicle listings. The dashboard is designed to help users explore pricing trends, market composition, vehicle characteristics, and regional distribution through interactive visualizations.

The project focuses on transforming raw marketplace data into meaningful business insights using Tableau Public and SQL.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze pricing trends across the Indian used car market.
Identify the most popular vehicle brands and body types.
Understand ownership patterns and vehicle age distribution.
Compare fuel types and transmission preferences.
Explore state-wise listing distribution.
Build a professional dashboard suitable for business users.

🔍 Key Insights
Hatchback vehicles dominate the used car market.
Petrol remains the most preferred fuel type.
Manual transmission vehicles account for the majority of listings.
Most vehicles fall within the Budget and Mid-Range price segments.
Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh contribute significantly to total listings.
Vehicles aged 8–12 years represent the largest portion of the market.
